Residential Projects

Residential Building Tel Aviv

Residential project in Tel Aviv, featuring 5 story buildings of approximately 2000 sqm with 3, 4, and 5 room apartments, as well as 9 story buildings of approximately 4000 sqm with similar unit types. All projects were fully designed and modeled in Revit, with an emphasis on accurate 3D modeling, interdisciplinary coordination, and efficient plan production from early design stages through to execution.

The Red House

Program: A family home that allows for the addition of rooms in stages, many sitting areas, balconies and a surrounding yard.

Plan: A dynamic design for a family home that seeks to bring the outdoors inside, as an integral part of the home. At the heart of the house is a patio that forms an intermediate area, dividing the ground floor into a public and private area. On the private side are the staircase leading to the master suite and a large roof terrace, 3 bedrooms, a common bathroom and a transparent study that opens onto the patio. The patio serves as a safe and protected space for children, while maintaining eye contact with the public areas.
