Commercial Projects

Residential Building Tel Aviv

Planning proposal for a security and municipal police wing building for the Petah Tikva Municipality, 2500 square meters. The selected plot is required to contain, in addition to the new security wing, the municipality's warehouse buildings, parking for police vehicles, seating areas and a garden with fitness facilities. The building itself is designed to create maximum intimacy while maintaining contact with the outside, thus allowing for a natural division into separate wings, including a classified wing, a shelter and a lodging and welfare wing, and each wing has a staff room for the welfare of the employees, with a view of the surrounding courtyard.

Galil Yam Kindergarten

Planning proposal for a kindergarten in the Galil Yam neighborhood in Herzliya. Planning 4 kindergarten classrooms on 2 floors while creating separate, naturally shaded courtyards, on the ground floor and the roof. Maximizing the area of the lot and adhering to the sides facing the street, creates a pleasant and aesthetic facade for pedestrians on the one hand and safe and intimate for children on the other. This makes it possible to protect the children without the need to erect fences and bars on all sides.
